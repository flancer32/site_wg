Launch of My Personal Website

Publication date: 2024-09-25

I once had a personal website before — in the Web1 era it was natural: your own pages, your own domain, everything under control. Later, with the advent of Web2, I switched to external platforms — Habr, GitHub, LinkedIn, Medium and others became my main points of presence.

Today I'm returning to a format where I once again control the content, structure, and architecture — but at a new level. This website is not just a showcase, but a working demonstration of my approaches to development and digital independence. In the spirit of Web3, it's built around transparency, automation, and open interaction with AI.

Here I share my experience, projects, and services in web development. I talk about how I create PWAs and SPAs, design architectures, work with custom Telegram bots, and integrate AI into everyday development.

The launch of this website is an important milestone in my public and professional development. Welcome!
