Website Migration to TeqCMS

Publication date: 2025-06-03

Today my personal website has been fully migrated to TeqCMS - my own minimalist CMS developed as part of the TeqFW architectural platform. Now the entire website operates on a unified system of templates, localization, and content generation, fully managed through Git and SSR.

TeqCMS allows storing content in files, automatically translating it using AI, and publishing as a static site without dependencies on databases or external services. This migration represents a logical step in the evolution of my infrastructure solutions.

The website has now become not just a showcase for my projects, but also a demonstration of TeqCMS capabilities. I plan to share implementation details, architecture, and workflows - as part of an open experiment on integrating AI into everyday development.
