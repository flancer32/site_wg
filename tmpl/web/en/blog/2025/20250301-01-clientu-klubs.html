Client Club: Customer Database for Domotehnika

Publication date: 2025-03-01

Implemented a small project on TeqFW for Latvian electronics store Domotehnika: Client Club — a lightweight web application for collecting and managing customer database.

The system helps accumulate contact details for subsequent use in marketing campaigns, loyalty programs and personalized offers. Users can register via phone or email and participate in loyalty campaigns.

Key features:

  • Registration via phone or email
  • GDPR-compliant consent management
  • Participation in campaigns and bonus accumulation
  • CRM tools: segmentation and contact export

The system is completely written in ES6 modules and built on modular monolith with late binding and DI architecture implemented in TeqFW.

If you're developing marketing or CRM solutions for retail — this stack is worth considering. No WordPress or Laravel — everything is built from scratch, tailored to the task.
