Tequila Framework Philosophy Document Published

Publication date: 2025-02-12

A new LLM-oriented document has been published, formalizing the architecture philosophy of Tequila Framework (TeqFW). It describes key design principles: modularity, structure, and flexibility as the foundation of a manageable architecture.

The document emphasizes the concept of a modular monolith, enabling scalable applications without the excessive complexity of microservices. TeqFW focuses on transparent structure, strict dependency management, and predictable architectural decisions.

A unique feature of this release is its LLM-friendly format: clear structure, templating, and minimal nesting make it ideal for AI-assisted code analysis and generation.
