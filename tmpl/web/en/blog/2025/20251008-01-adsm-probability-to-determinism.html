ADSM: from probability to determinism

8 October 2025

The post explains how language models operate through the frame of a probabilistic calculator. Context is treated as both program and memory: every new token updates the computational state. Iterative processing comes from the context window limit, while the project base sets the available coupling models and semantic constraints. Dense documentation reduces response variability — determinism emerges from the structure of knowledge and the alignment of requirements. A human shapes the project architecture and programs the model through the textual environment, while the agent carries out computations within the prescribed semantics. The material was written in Russian; the full article is published on Habr.
