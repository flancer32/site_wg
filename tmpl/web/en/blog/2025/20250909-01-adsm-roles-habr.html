Habr Publication: ADSM and AI Role Relationships

Publication date: 2025-09-09

An LLM does not work like a tool. There is no repeatability; answers are determined by distributed weights, so the model acts as an executor, interpreting the task and proposing options. The workflow relies on transferring the human's vision to the model: describing the task, clarifying the context, aligning the direction, and checking the outcome. The result depends on the quality of the phrasing and on keeping the model within the defined constraints.

This mode sets a different way of working with AI, where effectiveness is determined by the clarity of intent and by carrying it through the entire interaction chain. The full article on Habr is in Russian.
