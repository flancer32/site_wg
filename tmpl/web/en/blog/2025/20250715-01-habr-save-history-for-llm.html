Publication on Habr: history of prompts to AI agents

Publication date: 2025-07-15

My article “Do you save the history of prompts to AI agents?” has been published on the Habr platform.

In the article, I share my experience of using OpenAI Codex and conclude that when developing with AI agents, the key artifact is not so much the code or documentation, but the instructions and the history of prompts. They reflect the tasks that were set and the results that were obtained.

I added a poll to the publication. Half of the participants (50%) answered that they keep the history of iterations, and another third (33%) found the idea interesting. This shows that keeping a record of interactions with AI is already in demand.
