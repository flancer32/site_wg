TeqFW Guide: DTO — a personal architecture assistant

February 17, 2026

I launched TeqFW Guide: DTO — a personal GPT assistant for working with TeqFW architecture. In this framework, a DTO has a strictly defined place and function, which differs from common interpretations. Developers join with their own experience and mental models, and for a team it matters to align those models around a single architectural baseline. The assistant is grounded in a fixed corpus of documents and system rules, provides stable interpretation, and conveys a coherent view of DTOs in TeqFW. It adapts the dialogue to the user’s level and helps align understanding without sacrificing architectural integrity. It’s a tool for onboarding and day-to-day work that helps keep a single architectural voice across the project.
