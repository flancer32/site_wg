My ADSM book in English is on Leanpub

April 22, 2026

The English version of my ADSM book is now on Leanpub. I translated it with a Codex agent, and it took about 10% of my weekly Codex quota on GPT Plus.

What made the translation straightforward was that I kept the original Russian manuscript in Markdown. That was the key factor, and the book is short, about 60 pages, so I could get through it quickly.

This follows the previous note about the Russian edition on Leanpub. The result feels solid enough, although I would not call myself a strong English writer. If any subscribers want a free copy, they can ask for it through my Telegram channels: ru, en, es.
