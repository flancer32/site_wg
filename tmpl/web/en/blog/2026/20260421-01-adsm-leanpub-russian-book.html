ADSM book published on Leanpub

April 21, 2026

My Russian-language book on ADSM is now published on Leanpub. The approach has matured to the point where its parts could be set down clearly in prose, one by one, without losing the shape of the whole.

The book offers a practical view of development with an AI agent. Documentation sets the working context, and code follows from that context. In this form, ADSM describes a controlled way of working: the human defines the meaning, constraints, and structure, and the agent turns them into code.
