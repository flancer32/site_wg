NutriLog Open Beta Launch

Publication date: 2025-04-01

I'm pleased to announce the launch of NutriLog — a personal AI assistant for calorie tracking, nutrition analysis, and weight goal management.

On April 1st, we started the open beta: the first 100 users received access with 3 months of free subscription. To attract this audience, I conducted a small experiment with Google Ads — directing limited but precisely targeted traffic.

NutriLog is built on TeqFW — a lightweight modular JavaScript platform I developed specifically for reliable architecture and managed development.

The system combines a GPT-like chat with external memory: users maintain conversational freedom while gaining structure — goals, progress tracking, and visualization. If you're interested in a self-directed approach to nutrition and weight control — try NutriLog. Your feedback is most welcome!
