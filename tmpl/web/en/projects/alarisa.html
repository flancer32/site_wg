Alarisa

A private digital counterpart that preserves context and intent across work with AI agents.

Why I am building it

Digital life is scattered across messages, notes, files, services, and short-lived chats. Context disappears, priorities drift, and an agent that acts without enough understanding can easily do the wrong thing. Alarisa is meant to keep useful context and continuity around one person’s real work, so the next step starts with more than a blank prompt.

One personEach private instance belongs to one person.
Durable contextConfirmed context persists beyond a single conversation.
Bounded actionThe person keeps authority over consequential work.

What it is meant to do

Alarisa will help clarify goals, keep related work connected over time, and prepare or delegate digital tasks through approved tools and agents. Consequential actions should be visible, bounded, and confirmed whenever authority or risk is unclear.

The project is built with my own TeqFW framework and ADSM methodology. They provide the engineering structure and living project context needed to develop an agent-based tool deliberately rather than as a collection of prompts.

Alarisa is still in development.
