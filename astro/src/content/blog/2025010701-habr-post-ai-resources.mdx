I published an article titled **"<a href="/posts/20250107-ai-resources-levels/">How AGI Will Make the Smart Richer and Everyone Else Poorer</a>"**
on [Habr](https://habr.com/). However, just 4-5 hours later, the article was removed due to the platform's policy
prohibiting the publication of fully AI-generated content.

What makes this surprising is that the article wasn’t entirely generated by AI—it was based on my own ideas, which I
asked AI to edit and style. This raises an important question: where should we draw the line between AI-assisted and
AI-generated content?

I believe this situation highlights the challenges society faces in adapting to new forms of collaboration with AI.
