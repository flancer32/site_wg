The document focuses on synchronizing the **cognitive context** between the audience and the documented subject. It introduces improved recommendations for structuring information at different levels, ensuring logical progression from general concepts to specifics.

The updated version also incorporates guidelines for **LLM-oriented formatting**, emphasizing Markdown structure, key information placement, and terminological consistency.

For those interested in improving documentation practices, the new version offers refined insights into content organization, reader immersion, and navigation accessibility.
