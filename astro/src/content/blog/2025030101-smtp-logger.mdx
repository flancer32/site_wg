Happy to share another small project built on **TeqFW**:
📦 **[Clientu Klubs](https://tc.klientuklubs.lv/app/about/)** — a lightweight web app developed for **Domotehnika**, a Latvian electronics store.
It helps collect and maintain a customer database for future marketing campaigns.

The main idea: help the retailer **build and maintain a direct marketing database** through customer interactions. The system encourages shoppers to register and participate in campaigns, making it easier to connect offers with real people — not just anonymous purchases.

### Key Features

- Simple user registration via phone or email
- GDPR-friendly consent tracking
- Campaign participation and bonus collection
- Internal CRM tools for segmenting and exporting contacts

The entire system is built with **modular ES6 architecture** and uses **TeqFW’s late binding and DI** system for flexibility and maintainability.

If you're building marketing tools or CRM-like systems for retail — this stack is worth considering.
And yes, it's 100% custom, no WordPress or Laravel here 😉
