I’m excited to announce the launch of **[NutriLog](https://nutrilog.app.wiredgeese.com/)** — a personal AI assistant designed to help you track calories, analyze your nutrition, and stay focused on your weight goals.

The open beta started on **April 1st**, with access granted to the first **100 early users**, who receive **3 months of free subscription**. To attract this initial group, I ran a small experiment with **Google Ads**, directing a limited but highly targeted stream of traffic to the project.

NutriLog is built on **[TeqFW](https://github.com/teqfw/di)** — a lightweight, modular JavaScript platform I developed for building maintainable web applications.

The system combines GPT-based chat with a structured data backend, offering a hybrid approach: conversational flexibility plus goal tracking and visual progress. If you’re interested in self-guided weight management with smart assistance, give NutriLog a try!

Feedback is very welcome!
