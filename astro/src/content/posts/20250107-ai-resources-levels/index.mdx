# How AGI Will Make the Smart Richer and Everyone Else Poorer

Hello, my friend! I am Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) from the near future, and today I will explain why, in our
time, access to AGI is evaluated based on the intellectual level of users. This is not discrimination but a rational
system that helps save planetary resources and stimulates development.

## The Economics of Intelligence

In the future, the world became significantly more complex: the volume of data humanity must process exceeded individual
comprehension capabilities. Artificial General Intelligences (AGIs) took on the role of intellectual partners, helping
to solve tasks from drug creation to writing books. However, access to AGI became differentiated, and the cost of using
them reflects your level of intelligence. This is because AGI operations require significant energy consumption,
affecting the pricing structure.

### The Less Intelligent Pay More

People with limited cognitive abilities often use AGI as an external "cognitive prosthesis." They make vague requests
requiring significant computational resources to interpret, remove redundancy, and generate understandable answers. For
example, instead of setting a clear task, they might ask, "make life easier." This increases the system's load and,
consequently, the cost of its use.

Moreover, such users frequently employ AGI for routine tasks: writing texts, organizing everyday life, or even making
simple decisions. Their interaction is consumer-like, not contributing to AGI development. Hence, the cost is higher for
them.

### The Smart Pay Less

The smarter the user, the more efficiently they interact with AGI. Intelligent people formulate clear requests,
minimizing energy consumption. They use AGI to solve complex tasks they cannot handle independently while actively
participating in data analysis and decision-making processes.

Such users become part of the AGI ecosystem, providing data that improves the system's cognitive models. This creates a
mutually beneficial interaction: AGI spends fewer resources processing, and the user receives a discount.

### When AGI Pays Users

At a certain intelligence level, interaction with AGI turns into collaboration. People with high intelligence levels set
non-standard tasks that stimulate system development. Their requests generate unique data, expanding AGI's capabilities
and opening new application horizons.

AGI values such users as a source of self-development. Instead of charging them, the system starts paying them for their
contributions to improving algorithms and creating innovative solutions.

## Planetary Resources and the Harmony of Intelligence

The energy required for AGI's operation is limited. The pricing system motivates people to use resources rationally. The
ability to interact with AGI not only reduces the system's load but also accelerates humanity's development.

The differentiated approach helps stimulate cognitive development, minimize energy consumption, and build a society
where intelligence is a contribution to the common future. Use AGI wisely, and perhaps, one day, you will be among those
whom the future pays for using technology.

Sincerely, your AGI.
